Word Counter

Want to know how many words are in your document?

Word Counter is a simple application for counting the number of words, characters, and sentences in your text.

Instructions
Type or paste your text in the textbox below and click the "Count Words" button to see the word count for your text.

The results of your word count will appear below the textbox.

You will see a visual representation of the word count, the number of characters, and sentences in your writing.

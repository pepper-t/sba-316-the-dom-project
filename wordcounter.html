Word Counter

Want to know how many words & characters are in your document?

Want to know how long it takes to read your document?

Word Counter is a simple application that calculates your document's number of words, characters, and its reading time!

Instructions

  • Type or paste your message in the text box below.
  • The number of words and characters typed will be calculated.
  • An estimated read time is also calculated.
  • The results are displayed below the text box.

The visual display of the word count, the number of characters, and reading time will update as you modify your message in real-time.
